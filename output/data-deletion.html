Data Deletion Instructions

Effective Date: March 21, 2026  |  Last Updated: March 21, 2026

Adsora OS respects your right to control your personal data. This page explains how you can request deletion of your data from our platform and what happens when you do.

How to Request Data Deletion

You can request deletion of your data through any of the following methods:

Option 1: Email Request

Send an email to privacy@adsora.com with the subject line "Data Deletion Request" and include:

  • Your full name
  • The email address associated with your Adsora OS account
  • Whether you want a full account deletion or deletion of specific data

Option 2: In-App Request

If you have access to the Adsora OS application, you can request data deletion through your account settings.

Option 3: Facebook Login Callback

If you signed up using Facebook Login, you can remove Adsora OS from your Facebook account settings under Settings > Security > Apps and Websites. This will trigger our data deletion process automatically.

What Gets Deleted

Upon receiving a valid deletion request, we will delete the following data:

  • Your account profile and authentication credentials
  • Stored Meta (Facebook) access tokens and ad account connections
  • Google service connections and stored tokens (Drive, Gmail, Calendar)
  • Telegram bot connections and chat history
  • Cached ad performance data and reports
  • AI conversation history and recommendation logs
  • Alert preferences and notification settings
  • Any ad creatives, copy, or landing pages stored within our system

Note: Deletion of your data from Adsora OS does not delete your ads, campaigns, or data stored directly on Meta's platforms, Google's platforms, or any other third-party service. You must manage that data directly through those platforms.

What We May Retain

Certain data may be retained even after a deletion request, as required by law or legitimate business needs:

  • Transaction and billing records (retained for tax and accounting compliance)
  • Data required to comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, or enforce agreements
  • Anonymized or aggregated data that cannot be used to identify you

Deletion Timeline

  1. Acknowledgment: We will confirm receipt of your request within 2 business days
  2. Verification: We may contact you to verify your identity before processing the request
  3. Processing: Your data will be deleted within 30 days of verification
  4. Confirmation: You will receive a confirmation email once deletion is complete

Important: Data deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. Once deleted, we cannot recover your account, historical reports, or any associated data. Please export any data you wish to keep before submitting a deletion request.

Revoking Third-Party Access

In addition to deleting data from Adsora OS, we recommend revoking access from the connected platforms:

  • Meta/Facebook: Settings > Security and Login > Apps and Websites > Remove Adsora OS
  • Google: myaccount.google.com/permissions > Remove Adsora OS
  • Telegram: Use the /stop or /clear command in the Adsora OS bot, or block the bot
