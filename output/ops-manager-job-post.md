# Operations Manager — Run the Machine So We Can Scale

**Adsora | Performance Growth Partner**
Remote | Base + Profit Share

---

## The Opportunity

Adsora is a performance marketing agency managing ~$500K/month in ad spend across Meta, with $100–200K/month in profit. We run affiliate lead gen campaigns in home services, insurance, legal, and other high-value verticals.

The founder currently runs operations, media buying, client relationships, creative direction, and strategy — all at once. That's the bottleneck.

We're hiring an Operations Manager to **own the day-to-day machine** so the founder can focus on growth, partnerships, and scaling into new channels. You will be the person who makes sure nothing falls through the cracks, campaigns are delivered on time, reporting is tight, and the business runs whether the founder is looking or not.

This is not a media buying role. This is the person who **runs the business.**

---

## Your Mission

Take over operational control of a $500K/month agency and build the systems, processes, and rhythm that allow us to scale to $1M–$2M/month in ad spend without breaking.

---

## What Success Looks Like

### First 10 Days — Learn the Machine
- Shadow the CEO for 4 hours a day — absorb every campaign, relationship, decision pattern, and workflow
- Map every active campaign, offer, ad account, and partner relationship into a single operational overview
- Audit the full tool stack (ClickFlare, GoHighLevel, Trello, Slack, Google Drive, Facebook Ads Manager) — know what's working, what's duct-taped, and what's missing
- Identify the top 5 operational bottlenecks and present a concrete plan to fix them
- Take over the weekly reporting cadence — pull data, flag issues, surface opportunities

### By 30 Days — Run the Machine
- CEO is out of the weeds — you own daily operations
- QA every campaign before it goes live: tracking verified, creatives approved, targeting confirmed, budgets correct
- Handle all ClickFlare tracking setup, postback configuration, and troubleshooting
- Manage the ad launch pipeline end-to-end without hand-holding
- Establish the daily and weekly operational rhythm: standups, performance reviews, creative pipeline check-ins
- Relationships with affiliate networks and creative partners (POWERK) transitioned to you
- First SOPs drafted for the highest-volume workflows (ad launch, campaign QA, reporting)

### By 90 Days — Own the Machine
- All core SOPs documented and enforced (ad launch, campaign setup, creative rotation, performance review, client onboarding)
- Daily and weekly rhythms running without founder involvement
- Campaign performance dashboards live and updated daily
- Coordinate media buyers — they have everything they need (creatives, landing pages, tracking, accounts) to ship fast
- You are the operational heartbeat of the company

### By 6 Months — Scale the Machine
- Operational capacity to support 2–3 media buyers running campaigns simultaneously
- Onboarding playbook for new media buyers — they're productive within 1 week
- Hiring and managing an EA or junior coordinator to handle admin tasks
- Financial operations: reconcile ad spend vs. payouts weekly, flag discrepancies, track profitability by offer and channel
- Proactively identify scaling opportunities and operational risks before they become problems

### By 12 Months — You're the COO
- The business runs at $3M+/month in spend with tight margins and clean operations
- You've added a few key players to the team
- Founder trusts you to make decisions up to a $100K threshold without approval

## What You Will Do Daily

**Campaign Operations**
- QA all campaigns before launch (tracking, creatives, targeting, budgets)
- Monitor campaign performance daily — flag anomalies, underperformers, and overspend
- Coordinate creative rotation: pull assets from Drive, ensure media buyers have fresh creatives
- Manage the ad launch pipeline: Trello card → creative → landing page → campaign → live

**Systems & Process**
- Build and maintain SOPs for every repeatable process
- Own the tech stack: ClickFlare, GoHighLevel, Trello, Slack, Google Drive, Facebook Ads Manager
- Set up and troubleshoot tracking, pixels, postbacks, and attribution
- Maintain campaign naming conventions and organizational standards across all ad accounts

**Reporting & Finance**
- Pull and compile daily/weekly/monthly performance reports
- Track spend vs. budget vs. payout across all offers and channels
- Reconcile affiliate network payouts with internal tracking
- Flag financial discrepancies immediately

**Communication & Coordination**
- Be the main point of contact for affiliate networks and partners
- Coordinate between media buyers, creative team, and founder
- Manage Slack communication — ensure nothing gets lost
- Run weekly team standups and operational reviews

**People & Scaling**
- Onboard new media buyers and contractors
- Hire and manage junior ops support as we grow
- Build the organizational structure needed to scale from $500K to $2M/month in spend

|---|---|

**What this looks like in practice:**

| Company Profit | Your Profit Share | Total Monthly Comp |
|---|---|---|
| $100K (today) | $0 | $5,000 |
| $150K | $1,500 | $6,500 |
| $200K | $3,000 | $8,000 |

**Why this structure:**
- The $5K base is competitive for a remote ops role and gives you stability
- You earn nothing extra until the company grows past its current baseline — your profit share is on growth you help create
- The tiers accelerate as you scale — the bigger the business gets, the more you make
- At $300K/month profit, you're making $13K/month ($156K/year). At $500K, you're at $25K/month ($300K/year). That's life-changing money and you'll never want to leave
- 90-day ramp before profit share kicks in — prove you can run the machine first

**Path to COO:** If you perform at an A-level for 12 months, we discuss a COO title, higher base, and potentially phantom equity or a formal profit share increase.
