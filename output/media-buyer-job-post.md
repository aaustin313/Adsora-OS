## What Success Looks Like

### First 30 Days
- Get one offer to breakeven or profitable with your ads and landing pages
- Pick 1–2 core offers and fully own them
- Set up campaigns, tracking, and postbacks in ClickFlare
- Create ads and build landing pages to hit the breakeven/profitability goal

### By 90 Days
- Consistently manage **$5K–$20K/day** in ad spend on your channel, hitting or beating agreed KPIs (payout targets, ROAS, CPL)
- Have at least **2 winning offers live** with stable spend and clear scaling plans
- Ship a minimum of **15–30 ads per day** (test video concepts, offers, hooks, ad copy, images, etc.)
- Build winning landers in GoHighLevel and keep them tested and updated

### By 12 Months
- Own a portfolio of offers spending **$30K–$100K/day** at healthy margins
- Hire a video editor to assist you with ads
- Recruit or refer another A-player media buyer
