The Philosophy of Offers




3 stages - creative stage, formulaic stage, process/systems stage.


Creative stage = 0-$250k
* Economics
* Intentional business models
* Philosophy of offers
Formulaic stage = $250k-$1m
Systems Stage = $1m+


What is an offer:  A value vehicle that gets someone an outcome in exchange for currency.


An outcome = result


Examples outcomes: 


* Make money
* Lose weight
* See better
* Control your blood sugar
* Train your dog
* Get the girlfriend
* Get your ex back
* Make money with crytpo


Two types of truths: Absolute truth and Realtive truth


“My truth” = relative truth = subjective truth


“The truth” = absolute truth = objective truth


Since we want to get people results:


Absolute truth / Objective truth = repeatable outcome based on variable inputs


Otherwise it’s not going to be repeatable. 


The absolute / objective truth has to be based on math and also backed out by math, it has to work for others.


This is why we compete on economics. The math backs out.


The development of the philosophy of your offer


1. You discover your principles
   1. What must be true to get the outcome your customers want
2. Then you develop your philosophy
3. Then you develop your process leads to the outcome
   1. Be careful because the process can change in time, pay attention to static / dynamic markets moel. 
4. Then you develop your product or products
   1. Pay attention to creative vs mechanical
5. Then you develop your messaging. 
6. Then you develop your marketing, because marketing is nothign more than a distribution of your messaging. 
7. This leads to conviction -> confidence -> and conversions. 


  



When you focus on 1st acquisition strategy, you can’t scale, because they dont’ come back to buy, since it’s marketing focused.


When you focus on 2nd, 3rd, 4th, 5th, you can scale.


Your marketing should convince them to buy ONCE, and your product shold convince them to buy AGAIN and AGAin and AGAIN.


When you have a philosophy, you can defend it against anyone and argue for it for your customers. It’s the truth and the way they get the out come. It’s not about being right, it’s about having the truth.


It’s about being RIGHT, now about the truth.


On Alen’s side, it’s about the TRUTH, not being right.


And as they…the truth is self evident.


Your copy can’t convert without confidence. The reason it hs confidence because it knows what it’s selling is better than anything and that it’s value is far higher than the money it will ask it.  


Because conviction > confidence. 


If you have conviction, you will convince others. But you can’t have conviction without 1st convincing yourself.


If you have to spend 3 months finding “the big idea” or “the hook” or “the mechanism” you’re trying to convince yourself that what you have is worth as hit.


Philosophy of offers lets you go from inside out, instead of outside in. You don’t have to search for it, it just is.
