Mindset #1 — Matchers and Mismatchers


* In order to perform at a very high level, you have to have a high level of emotional stability and control.
* Largely it has to do with being able to detach your responsibility from other people's responsibility
* The 1st thing that I look for in someone who went from being a high performer to a low performer is “what changed?”
* Other people's beliefs will diffuse and become your beliefs
* Beliefs tend to get absorbed
* If you hang out or are around other people and they say SOMETHING and you AGREE with them, then your subconscious mind will ABSORB that belief
   * Instead of telling them to F off
* If you don’t want to offend someone, you tend to be a matcher
* Someone says something, and you agree with them
* Mismatchers always disagree, they always contradict you
* When people are matches they build rapport really well
* When people are mismatchers they break rapport
* The ONLY way to be around mismatchers is to AGREE with them
   * But if you do AGREE with them, then by default you absorb their belief systems
      * Including limitations, problems, issues, world view, etc…
* If you hang around matchers, then know that you are easily influencing them because they are absorbing YOUR belief system
   * If you express limitations, problems, etc…matchers will deepen those beliefs

Increasing Beliefs


There is a level of skepticism increasing in the  marketplace


So the higher the skepticism, the higher the resistance to buying from us, so we have to know how to lower it, and to do that we increase beliefs.


* What I see in the world is that production is increasing and consumption is decreasing.
* The reason production is increasing is because the cost of production is going DOWN, and the cost of distribution is zero.
   * It doesn’t take much to make a post on FB, Twitter, IG, YouTube or TikTok
* Whenever there is an economic incentive to create content, the amount of content that gets created increases exponentially.
   * That’s what’s happening now
   * People figured out how to turn content into money
* Monetization is commercialization and whenever you commercialize anything then you enter scales of production 
* This completely destroys the trust in the ecosystem
* The internet before AdSense and the internet AFTER AdSense is very different
* These days everything on Google feels generic
* As you decrease the value of content you increase the value of the paid stuff, hence why people continued to pay Google
* Same thing happened to YouTube circa 2016
* Then content creator as a profession came about and then of course YouTube went to shit
* People create courses and such and more and more is created because there is no cost to create
* The only cost are ads
* When people are over exposed to something the novelty of it all decrease and the trust decreases quite a bit
* As trust decreases, the level of skepticism in the market increase
* Skepticism is measured by uncertainty
   * I’m not sure about that
   * I don’t know
   * Ehhhh maybe
   * I need to know more
* What is the level of skepticism in the market?
* We have to be skeptical of our own marketing in order to overcome the level of skepticism in the market
* If they suspect it’s bullshit, so do you
* If they think it’s great, so do you
* You have to match their level of thinking to get THROUGH their level of thinking
* A LOT of the mismatch between you and the market is that you do to match skepticism.
* How do you detect skepticism? You focus on what they question
* You  just Google famous people in the niche and then type in “scam” and that will lead you to a level of skepticism and pay attention to what are they saying and questions
* You have to have the ability recognize difference is the key to be successful 
* If the market is skeptical, you have to talk about the things the market is skeptical about
* You cannot ignore it
* You have to match the level of skepticism be it in your ads, emails, sales copy, upsells, etc…
* You have to match it and reframe it in your direction
* In this whole process of matching skepticism and beliefs there are 3 levels of thinking
* There are 3 levels of beliefs
   * Level 1 — Irrational Beliefs
   * Level 2—  Rational Beliefs
   * Level 3 — Direct Beliefs
* Some markets are irrational, some are rational and some are direct
* Desire tends to distort reality, the more desire there is the more irrational beliefs become
* You can see this is true in real estate, crypto and most recently NFTs
   * The desire was to greatly profit from the opportunity of stupid JPGs
   * It distorted the reality so much so that you could not tell people a stupid JPG was with $2m and thus they were very irrational
* Irrationality arises out of when there is great desire and not much stops to limit the distortion of that perception
* The easiest money you’ll ever make is when the market is irrational 
* There are 3 levels of beliefs
   * Level 1 — Irrational Beliefs (BELIEF) 
      * Largely dream state
      * Irrational faith
      * Belief in something that someone told you but you don’t understand the process or the rationality behind it
      * Don’t walk under a ladder it’s bad luck
      * If a black cat walks across the street, don’t walk over that line it’s bad luck
      * You believe it and you have no idea why that is the case
         * Which may be true or fale
         * And you have no way of knowing weather it’s true or false
      * Irrational beliefs / faith will arise when there are ENOUGH people telling them the same thing
      * This is sociology at play
      * Tulip Mania (1600s)
      * Beanie Babies
      * NFTs
      * Crypto currencies
      * Certain biz-ops
      * Etc…
      * Rationality is being able to explain something front to back without resorting to “it just is” claims
      * When someone comes to me to fix an offer, I look at this hierarchy to see how to increase the level of belief. 
      * Talk about how other people are doing it
      * Talk about how other people are succeeding
      * Talk about how everyone else is going in but you
         * Crypto offer “Everyone is getting rich but you”
      * If you have a group of people telling you something, then you do not need to verify it because you assume that others have verified it already
      * Advertorials and FLOGS generate irrational beliefs


   * Level 2 —  Rational Beliefs (BELIEF)
      * Largely awakening state
      * Something that you cannot know directly on your own, but it makes sense
      * A lot of the truth vs trust stuff we use hits rational beliefs
         * The origin of TRUTH 
      * Also why superstructures work so well
      * You don’t know that atoms exist or that electrons exist
      * Yet you use electricity and that concept makes sense
      * Rationality is something that can be explained with evidence that you have direct experience with
      * In order to affect rational beliefs in somebody we have to be AWARE of what direct experiences do they have?
      * AC model is built on this
         * I don’t know enough about this but there is enough that I do know about where I do believe you
      * A lot of this is educational marketing


   * Level 3 — Direct Beliefs (KNOWING)
      * They’ve awaken state
      * The things that you can actually see, experience and perceive directly
      * If you can actually SEE atoms it’s not irrational or rational it’s direct
      * This is a state of KNOWING
      * All infomercials are direct beliefs
         * LOOK AT THIS THING
         * It’s never about LET ME TELL YOU ABOUT THIS THING
* Irrational beliefs are never good, we need to generate rational beliefs and do our best to turn rational beliefs into DIRECT PERCEPTION
   * This direct perception becomes what is a state of KNOWING
* The reason for this process is very simple, and that is because belief systems are safety systems and if you KNOW then that means the safety system is at 100% functionality.
* When working on offers, optimizing them or ads or anything else, we must look at and see where the market is in terms of skepticism, and what level of belief they’re in
* Scott came to me and was stuck at $100k / month for a few years
   * Joined other programs, etc…couldn’t breakthrough $100k / month
   * I took him from $100k a month to $3,000,000 / month in about 4-5 weeks
* When markets mature they go full on direct beliefs
