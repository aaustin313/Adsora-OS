What are the Avatars of yourself? They’re all ME’s
IE: myself - business michele, friend michele, wild me, coaching michele, secret weapon michele, savage michele, ME’s




Questions to evaluate people (on dates, business setting, friendship, etc):
1 - Who are 3 people you admire & why?
2 - What are 3 character traits of each person you admire and why?
3 - Who do you have contempt for & why?


Example:
1. 3 people that you strongly admire - must be real people 
   1. Tarantino
   2. William golding
   3. Faulkner
   4. Francis fordicopola - filmed God father, nick cages uncle
2. What are 3 character traits of the people you admire (driven is a character trait, not rich)
   1. Tenacious
   2. Brilliant
   3. Driven
   4. Tenacious, Brilliant, Masterful
   5. Creative


This tells you what they value and the characteristics and traits they embody.
