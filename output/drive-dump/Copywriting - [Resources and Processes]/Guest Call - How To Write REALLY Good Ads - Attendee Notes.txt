Purpose of an Ad 
Prime them to click, then buy 
1. Stop the scroll, capture curiosity / intrigue 
a. Headline 
b. Ad opener / “Alternative Headline” 
c. First 3 seconds of video / Image(s) 
2. Build intent / desire / establish beliefs 
a. Headline 
b. Rest of the ad copy 
c. Rest of the video / Images

● What the “thing” is 
● Mechanism / Steps / Process
