(Optional) Pattern Interrupt (0-5s):
* What’s the pattern interrupt?
* It’s essential to make sure this is paid off somewhere in the copy. Doesn’t have to be immediate, but can be.
* Ideas:
* Narrow their attention on an object/prop or something on screen
* Flash something on screen that’s confusing
* Make a shocking statement
* Allude to something that means impending danger
* Drop a BIG hook (this is also kinda the next step but sometimes it can double as a good attention grabber)
* Unique
* Useful
* Ultra-Specific
* Urgent
* Trendy
* Movement / Fast Pace
* (A great way to START videos is by walking towards the camera)
* Could also flash a few things on the screen quickly.
Quick Hook (0-5s)
* Examples
* “If you’re experiencing XYZ problem…”
* “If you’re [insert ideal avatar]...”
* “If you want to [insert desire]...
* All you’re really doing here is calling out to the audience. You can call out to:
* WHO they are (if you’re a realtor)
* Their current circumstances (if ios14 has decimated your ad tracking…)
* Their problems (if your sales team is underperforming)
* Their desires (if you want to increase profit from ads)
Big Claim / Promise (5-20s):
* Here what you’re going to do is state the ONE BIG BENEFIT you can help your clients achieve.
* You could also do 2-3 BIG BENEFITS.
* Ideally… you can modify these based on what headlines / angles are working best in ads. It will  take some research to dial in.
* Key points:
* Keep them short
* STATE the benefit then expand on it briefly by explaining your part of your USP
* Think about your USP as:
* Why is using your product/service exponentially more effective than the other options they have to achieve the same desire?
* Other options may include:
* Direct Competitors who offer a similar service
* DIY
* Indirect competitors who offer a different service BUT something that could still get them the same result.
* Don’t mull on here, by quick and too the point.
Big Guarantee
* “You achieve XYZ result OR…. [insert your guarantee]”
* Saying “don’t pay” is an easy one… but there’s a lot of things here.
* “We’ll keep working with you - in perpetuity - until you do”
* “We’ll buy your amazon business from you”
* “We’ll pay you $$$”
* Get creative with it.
Big Proof / Track Record (20-45s):
* This is PROOF that’s paying off the big promise
* It also can be used to build authority in your expert domain
* In my example we have a crazy track record of industry names… but if I didn’t have that I’d rapid fire 5 case studies with quick before / afters in a specific time frame.
Soft CTA:
* See the demo - very simple
Mechanism:
* This could also be your USP… or a bit of both.
* Here (similar to in the section with the promise where I mentioned to briefly expand on your USP) you want to explain why your option is the best compared to ANY other option they have in terms of achieving said result.
* You could do this as 2-3 reasons why OR you could explain ONE BIG REASON why (which is more like your mechanism)
* Think of your mechanism as the “simueltaneous explanation of why everything they tried in the past has failed and why this is going to be different”
* Most everyone trying to achieve result is doing this… (the problem)
* Here’s the problem with that…(reason why that problem is a problem)
* Which ultimately means… (ultimate consequence)
* So instead, we do… (your specific methodology)
* Which allows us to… (get benefit)
* Which ultimately means… (benefit of that benefit)
* The more sophisticated / competitive your market is the more I would focus on ONE key reason (mechanism).
