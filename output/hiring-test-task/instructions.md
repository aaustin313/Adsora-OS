# Marketing Tech Assistant — Test Task

## The Scenario

You just started at a performance marketing agency. Your manager sends you this message on a Monday morning:

---

> "Hey — I need you to take a look at things while I'm in meetings all day. Here's what's going on:
>
> 1. **Campaign performance:** We spent $23k last week across home insurance, Camp Lejeune (mass tort), and a newsletter growth campaign. I attached the data export. I need you to tell me what's working, what's not, and what you'd do about it. Be specific — I want actual recommendations, not 'monitor and optimize.'
>
> 2. **The Camp Lejeune landing page is broken:** It was cloned from our home insurance page last month and never properly updated. Pull it up, find everything wrong with it, and fix it. The file is `camp-lejeune-lp.html`.
>
> 3. **Tracking gap:** Check the tracking doc. There's a discrepancy between ClickFlare and Facebook on home insurance leads. I need you to tell me what's probably causing it and how you'd fix it.
>
> 4. **Write me 3 new headlines** for the Camp Lejeune campaign. The UGC testimonial ad is crushing it at $35 CPL while the other two are above $225. What's working about that one, and give me 3 new headlines that follow the same pattern.
>
> I need all of this by end of day. Don't ask me questions — figure it out."

---

## Files Included

| File | What It Is |
|------|-----------|
| `campaign-data.csv` | Last week's Facebook Ads performance data across all campaigns |
| `camp-lejeune-lp.html` | The Camp Lejeune landing page (broken — needs fixing) |
| `tracking-setup.md` | Current tracking and pixel configuration |

## What to Deliver

Send back **one document** (Google Doc, PDF, or email) with these sections:

### Part 1: Campaign Analysis
- What's working and what's not — by campaign, ad set, and ad level
- Specific actions: what to kill, what to scale, what to test next
- Show your math. We want to see how you think, not just your conclusions.

### Part 2: Landing Page Fixes
- Your fixed `camp-lejeune-lp.html` file (attached)
- A list of every issue you found and how you fixed it
- Note: there are problems across content, compliance, tracking, form setup, and code. Some are obvious, some are not.

### Part 3: Tracking Diagnosis
- What's most likely causing the ClickFlare vs. Facebook lead discrepancy?
- How would you investigate and fix it?
- Any other tracking issues you notice in the setup?

### Part 4: Headlines
- 3 new Camp Lejeune ad headlines
- Brief explanation of why the UGC testimonial ad is outperforming

## Rules

- Use any tools you want — AI, Google, dev tools, whatever. We don't care how you get the answer, we care if the answer is right.
- Time limit: **2 hours**. This is intentionally more than one person can perfectly finish. We want to see how you prioritize.
- Don't overthink formatting. Clear > pretty.

## What We're Evaluating

| Skill | What We're Looking For |
|-------|----------------------|
| Analytical thinking | Can you read data, find the signal, and make a decision? |
| Technical ability | Can you fix real code, spot tracking issues, understand pixels? |
| Marketing knowledge | Do you understand CPL, ROAS, audience targeting, compliance? |
| Copywriting instinct | Can you identify what makes an ad work and replicate the pattern? |
| Prioritization | Given too much to do, what do you tackle first? |
| Communication | Can you explain your thinking clearly and concisely? |
| Independence | Can you figure things out without hand-holding? |

Good luck.
