Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 21, 2026  |  Last Updated: March 21, 2026

Adsora OS ("we," "us," or "our") operates as an advertising management platform that connects to Meta (Facebook/Instagram) advertising services. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our application.

1. Information We Collect

Account & Authentication Data

  • Name, email address, and profile information provided during registration
  • Meta (Facebook) account credentials and access tokens obtained through OAuth authentication
  • Telegram user ID and chat information (for alert delivery)

Advertising Data

  • Meta Ad Account information (account IDs, campaign structures, ad sets, ads)
  • Ad performance metrics (spend, impressions, clicks, conversions, CPL, CTR, CPA, ROAS)
  • Ad creative content (images, videos, copy) stored in or retrieved from connected services
  • Campaign budgets and scheduling information

Connected Service Data

  • Google Drive files and documents you authorize us to access (ad creatives, swipe files)
  • Gmail messages you authorize us to access (affiliate communications)
  • Google Calendar events you authorize us to access
  • Newsletter platform data (subscriber counts, open rates, click rates) from connected services such as Beehiiv

Usage & Technical Data

  • Log data (IP address, browser type, access times, pages viewed)
  • Device information and operating system
  • Actions taken within the application (commands issued, features used)

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Ad Management: To create, launch, monitor, pause, and optimize advertising campaigns on Meta platforms on your behalf
  • Performance Analysis: To generate reports, flag underperforming ads, and recommend optimizations based on your campaign data
  • Alerts & Notifications: To send you real-time alerts via Telegram, email, or in-app notifications about campaign performance, budget thresholds, and recommended actions
  • Reporting: To compile weekly and on-demand performance reports aggregating data from your connected advertising and newsletter platforms
  • AI-Powered Recommendations: To provide business advisory insights, copywriting suggestions, and strategic recommendations based on your data
  • Service Improvement: To maintain, improve, and develop the functionality of our platform

3. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We may share information in the following limited circumstances:

  • Meta Platforms: We transmit data to Meta's APIs as necessary to manage your advertising campaigns (e.g., creating ads, setting budgets, retrieving metrics)
  • Connected Third-Party Services: We exchange data with services you explicitly connect (Google Drive, Gmail, Telegram, Beehiiv, etc.) solely to provide the features you request
  • AI Processing: Campaign data and messages may be processed by third-party AI services (such as Anthropic's Claude) to generate recommendations and responses. This data is not used to train AI models.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose information if required by law, regulation, legal process, or governmental request
  • Business Transfers: In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred as a business asset

4. Meta Platform Data

Our use of information received from Meta APIs adheres to the Meta Platform Terms and Developer Policies, including:

  • We only request permissions necessary to provide our advertising management services
  • We do not use Meta data for purposes unrelated to the services you have requested
  • We do not share Meta user data with third parties except as described in this policy
  • We delete Meta data upon your request or when it is no longer needed for the services
  • We comply with all applicable Meta data use restrictions and special ad category requirements

5. Data Security

We implement reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect your information, including:

  • Encryption of data in transit (TLS/SSL) and at rest
  • Secure storage of API keys, tokens, and credentials using environment variables and encrypted secrets management
  • Access controls limiting data access to authorized personnel only
  • Regular security reviews of our codebase and infrastructure

No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Data Retention

  • We retain your account data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services
  • Ad performance data is retained for reporting and analysis purposes for the duration of your account
  • Access tokens are refreshed and old tokens are discarded per OAuth best practices
  • Upon account deletion, we will delete or anonymize your data within 30 days, except where retention is required by law

7. Your Rights & Choices

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal data, subject to legal retention requirements
  • Revoke Access: Disconnect any third-party service (Meta, Google, Telegram, etc.) at any time through your account settings or the respective platform's settings
  • Opt Out of Alerts: Disable or customize notifications and alert preferences at any time
  • Data Portability: Request an export of your data in a commonly used format

8. Children's Privacy

Adsora OS is not intended for use by individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we learn we have collected data from a child under 18, we will delete that information promptly.

9. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your country of residence. These countries may have different data protection laws. We take appropriate safeguards to ensure your data remains protected in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the updated policy with a new "Last Updated" date. Your continued use of Adsora OS after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
