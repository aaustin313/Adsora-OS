# Reporting Rules

## Format
- Lead with metrics, then analysis, then recommendations
- Keep reports concise and scannable
- Use tables for comparing campaigns/ad sets
- Always include date range and comparison period

## Weekly Report Structure
1. **Top-line numbers:** Total spend, total leads, blended CPL
2. **By vertical:** Breakdown per vertical (home services, mass torts, etc.)
3. **Winners:** Top 3 performing ads/campaigns and why
4. **Losers:** Bottom 3 and recommended action (pause, iterate, test new creative)
5. **Newsletter metrics:** Beehiiv open rate, CTR, subscriber growth
6. **Next week:** Budget recommendations, tests to run, creatives to launch

## Data Sources
- Facebook Ads: spend, impressions, clicks, CTR, CPL, CPA, leads
- Beehiiv: subscribers, open rate, click rate, revenue
- Affiliate networks: conversion data, payouts (when available)
