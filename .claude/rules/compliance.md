# Compliance & Ad Policy

## Facebook Ads Policies
- No misleading claims in ad copy
- No before/after images without proper context
- No discrimination in targeting (housing, employment, credit)
- Special Ad Categories required for housing-related lead gen
- Mass tort ads may require legal disclaimers

## Lead Gen Compliance
- All landing pages must have privacy policy
- TCPA compliance for lead forms (consent language)
- CAN-SPAM compliance for email newsletters
- Honor opt-out requests immediately

## Affiliate Network Rules
- Follow each network's specific compliance requirements
- Track which offers are approved for which traffic sources
- Never send traffic to paused or expired offers
